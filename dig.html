Forbes 2021 Special Edition Disney Cover

1431px x 1752px Digital

This is a satirical Forbes cover done in Adobe Photoshop for a Digital Media class. For the cover, I referenced other Forbes covers, most heavily Elizabeth Holmes' special edition and Bill Gates' November 2011 cover. It's meant to be a commentary on the ridiculous amount of power Disney is developing.

Ariana Grande in Times Square

1000px x 667px Digital

This is another satirical piece. It is a glitch piece made through a combination of Adobe Photoshop and Audacity. It is a commentary on "influencer culture" and the way it distorts and disorients our vision of ourselves and each other. As Ariana Grande is often debated over her use of photoshop and plastic surgery, I thought she'd be a good fit.
