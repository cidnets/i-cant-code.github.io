A drawing of my older sister with her son. I happened to take a candid of her rocking him to sleep one night, and I liked the image so much that I wanted to draw it. I was more focused on capturing the mood of the scene rather than drawing them accurately.

Self-Portrait 2

11in x 14in Graphite

A self-portrait done for a drawing class using a mirror as reference. I've never been particularly good at drawing self-portraits (I find spending that much time alone with myself a bit stressful, to be honest), but I like the way this one turned out. I wasn't really drawing it with anything in mind, I was just trying to capture myself as accurately as I could in the moment. The tone of the piece came about on its own.

Self-Portrait 1
